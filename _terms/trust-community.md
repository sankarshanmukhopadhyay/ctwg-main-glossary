# trust-community

## Definition

## Notes
- A trust community may be large or small, formal or informal. In a formal trust community, the set of policies and rules governing behavior of members are usually published in a governance framework or trust framework. In an informal trust community, the policies or rules governing the behavior of members may be tribal knowledge.
