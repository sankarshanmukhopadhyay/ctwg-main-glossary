# Governance-Aware Glossary Extension Model

## Purpose

The extension model defines how ordinary glossary terms become governance-executable term artifacts without losing readability for standards authors, implementers, and reviewers.

The current model is no longer a sidecar profile. The authoritative extension shape is embedded directly in each YAML term under `glossary/terms/` and validated by `schemas/governance-term.schema.json`.

## Design goals

- preserve human-readable definitions;
- expose authority, delegation, revocation, lifecycle, evidence, and control-plane semantics;
- support deterministic generation of pages and machine-readable bundles;
- provide quality signals for maintainers;
- avoid overstating compliance or certification; and
- keep downstream consumers aligned to a stable validation contract.

## Extension fields

| Field | Meaning |
|---|---|
| `governance_profile` | Descriptive, governance-supporting, or core-operational classification. |
| `governance.authority_scope` | Scope in which the term may affect authority or recognition. |
| `governance.delegation_mode` | Whether authority is direct or directly/constrainedly delegated. |
| `governance.revocation_supported` | Whether revocation semantics apply. |
| `governance.lifecycle_states` | Lifecycle states relevant to the term. |
| `governance.execution_role` | Design-time, runtime, or hybrid relevance. |
| `governance.control_plane_role` | Whether the term is a reference term or decision-plane component. |
| `governance.enforcement_points` | Places where governance becomes operational. |
| `assurance.evidence_artifacts` | Evidence expected when the term is used operationally. |
| `assurance.assurance_level_hint` | Informative, AL1+, or AL2+ term-level assurance relevance. |
| `assurance.auditability` | Basic, moderate, or high auditability expectation. |
| `control_plane.decision_points` | Decisions affected by the term. |
| `control_plane.accountable_entity` | Entity accountable for operation or interpretation. |
| `control_plane.evidence_produced` | Evidence generated by the relevant decision or process. |

## Extension discipline

The extension model is intentionally conservative. It should make terms more useful to downstream tooling without turning glossary entries into legal rules or conformance claims.

When in doubt, prefer a lower assurance hint and a clearer source note over an inflated operational classification.
